Touch is great, Handsfree is better.


Handsfree video player allows you to play, pause, rewind and fast-foward any youtube video.

It is built on top of the xLabs gaze SDK, which has the capability to track head pose and eye gaze using ordinary webcams.

In order to use the handsfree video player, you need to install xLabs first .

Why handsfree?

Touch is greate, Handsfree is better.

Ever tried eating pizza and control a video player? Messy. Handsfree video player introduces an often overlooked control modality: head gestures. It's intuitive and unobtrusive. Give it a shot!

What is xLabs?

xLabs a SDK that tracks head pose and eye gaze using only ordinary webcams. No other hardware is required. You can find some more apps built on top of the SKD here

How to control video play back?

After installing the xLabs and browser extension, restart your browser. You can click here to check if you have installed it correctly.

Open the player, paste the YouTube video address into the box and click Load.

Tilt your head slowly left to rewind, right to fast-forward. Playback stops when you are not facing the screen.
